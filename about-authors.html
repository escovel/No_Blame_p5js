About the Authors

Tyler Carter is a poet and academic based in Kunshan, China. His poems, essays, and sound compositions have been published in various US based literary journals such as Fence, Denver Quarterly, textsound, Word For/Word, and VOLT. He also does academic research in comparative rhetoric and writing pedagogy. Currently he is an Assistant Professor of Rhetoric and Writing at Duke Kunshan University.

Eric Goddard-Scovel is an artist and poet based in Saint Louis, Missouri. His creative work is mostly centered in digital methods, including creative coding, text generation, natural language processing, sampling, digital synthesis, signal processing, etc. You may read his poetry chapbooks composed with Gnoetry 0.2 interactive poetry composition software at Beard of Bees Press, and you can find out more about his work and thoughts at ericgoddardscovel.com.
